Senior Baker
Job Description
  • Responsible on all bakeries preparation & production arrangement.
  • Measure ingredients or substances to be used in production processes.
  • Adapt the quantity and quality of ingredients to match the amount of items to be processes.
  • Check the quality of raw materials to ensure that standards and specifications are met.
  • Knowledgeable using, setting time and speed controls for all equipment and machines according to instructions.



Job Requirements
Min 3-5 years relevant experience in bakery field.
Customer service oriented.
Friendly and out going personality.
Pleasant personality with good problem solving skills.
Team player who is highly independent self motivated, result oriented and innovative.
Able to work shifts, weekend and public holidays.

Baker Trainees
Job Description
  • Helping existing baker by collecting necessary ingredients and reorder stock
  • Helping the baker with cooking processes.
  • Responsible for cleaning,organizing and checking product quality.
  • Learn to maintain high standard of food quality and consistency.
  • Pack and display bread in an organized manner.
  • Report to baker or senior baker.



Job Requirements
Baking / Food Preparation experience is desirable (not essential).
Full Training will be provided.
Work well within a team.
Ability to work in a physically demanding environment.
Good communication skills.
